Airdrop Wallet Guide

What is an Airdrop?

An airdrop is a method used by cryptocurrency projects to distribute free tokens or coins to the community. This helps in:

  • Promoting a new cryptocurrency.
  • Rewarding early adopters and active community members.
  • Encouraging engagement through simple tasks like signing up or following on social media.

Is an Airdrop Free?

Yes! Airdrops are free to join. You may, however, need to complete certain tasks (e.g., registering, following accounts, or sharing posts) to qualify for rewards.

Which Wallets Can We Use?

To receive your airdrop rewards, you’ll need a crypto wallet. Below are the up-to-date steps for setting up popular wallets on your PC:

MetaMask (Browser Extension)

  1. Visit metamask.io/download.
  2. Select the version for your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Brave, or Edge) and install the extension.
  3. Click “Get Started” and choose “Create a Wallet.”
  4. Set a strong password and securely save your 12-word seed phrase (write it down and store it offline).
  5. Confirm your seed phrase as prompted to finish the setup.

Phantom (Browser Extension)

  1. Go to phantom.app/download.
  2. Download and install the extension for your browser.
  3. Launch Phantom, then click “Create New Wallet.”
  4. Set a strong password and carefully save your 12-word seed phrase.
  5. Confirm your seed phrase when prompted to complete the setup.

Trust Wallet (Mobile App)

Note: Trust Wallet is primarily designed for mobile devices. For PC usage, consider an emulator or alternative browser wallets.

  1. Download Trust Wallet from the official website or your device’s app store.
  2. Open the app and tap “Create a New Wallet.”
  3. Agree to the terms, set a secure password, and write down your 12-word seed phrase.
  4. Confirm your seed phrase to finalize the setup.

No PC? Use Your Phone

If you’re on a mobile device, you can install these wallets directly or use Yandex Browser for extensions:

Option 1: Install Wallets Directly on Mobile

MetaMask (Mobile)

  1. Open the Play Store (Android) or App Store (iOS) and download the official MetaMask app.
  2. Launch the app and tap “Get Started.”
  3. Select “Create a New Wallet,” set a strong password, and agree to the terms.
  4. Your 12-word seed phrase will be revealed—write it down and store it safely.
  5. Confirm your seed phrase as instructed to complete the setup.

Phantom (Mobile)

  1. Download Phantom Wallet from the Play Store (Android) or App Store (iOS).
  2. Open the app and tap “Create New Wallet.”
  3. Set a secure password and tap “Continue.”
  4. Write down your 12-word seed phrase and store it securely.
  5. Confirm your seed phrase when prompted, then tap “Finish.”

Trust Wallet (Mobile)

  1. Download Trust Wallet from your device’s app store.
  2. Open the app and tap “Create a New Wallet.”
  3. Agree to the terms, set a strong password, and securely store your 12-word seed phrase.
  4. Confirm your seed phrase to complete the setup.

Option 2: Use Yandex Browser for Extensions

  1. Download Yandex Browser from your device’s app store.
  2. Open the browser, tap the menu (three dots), and select “Extensions.”
  3. Search for MetaMask or Phantom extensions and install them.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to create your wallet.
  5. Note: Trust Wallet is available only as a mobile app.
