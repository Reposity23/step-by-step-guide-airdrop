Nomisma

For Phones:

  1. Find the link in the Telegram channel or copy this one: https://nomisma.network/quests?invite=EjbMQtEy
  2. Click or copy the link and sign up.
  3. Use your MetaMask to sign up. Open your MetaMask wallet app and click the "Discover" button.
  4. Paste the copied link from the Telegram channel.
  5. Connect your wallet by finding the wallet icon and confirming the connection. MetaMask will prompt you to confirm. If unsuccessful, try again (3-4 times may be needed).
  6. After signing up, scroll down and complete all the tasks.
  7. Remember: More points equal a bigger Nomisma airdrop!

For PC:

  1. Make sure you have downloaded the MetaMask extension. Visit: MetaMask Setup
  2. Find the wallet icon and click Connect. It may take 3-4 tries for it to work.
  3. Scroll down to complete the task.

Mesh Chain

For PC:

  1. Link: https://app.meshchain.ai?ref=5AYIBFFTW92J
  2. Click Sign in with Google and choose your account.
  3. On your dashboard, click Install Browser Extension to be redirected to the Chrome Web Store and download the Mesh Chain extension.
  4. Locate the extension in your Chrome browser.
  5. Pin the Mesh Chain extension and click it to open. When prompted, click Log In and then click the extension again.
  6. Optionally, connect your Telegram node by clicking Set up Telegram Node in the dashboard.
  7. Every 2 hours, claim your points either via the extension or the Telegram app.

For Mobile:

  1. Download the Yandex Browser.
  2. Sign up in Mesh Chain using this link: https://app.meshchain.ai?ref=5AYIBFFTW92J (use your Google account and click Sign In).
  3. Search for the Mesh Chain extension and download it.
  4. Or copy this link and paste it in your Yandex browser.
  5. Tap the three dots next to the URL bar.
  6. Scroll down until you find the extension, then click Mesh Node. When prompted to sign in, click to continue.
  7. Also, connect your Telegram node by clicking Set up Telegram Node in the dashboard.
  8. Claim your points every 2 hours using the extension or Telegram app.

Unich

  1. Click this link: Unich Airdrop Signup
  2. Create an account using your personal email.
  3. After signing up, scroll down and complete the tasks to earn points.

3dos (PC Only)

  1. Click this link: 3dos Registration
  2. Sign up for an account using your Gmail.
  3. After signing up, scroll down and copy your API key.
  4. Download the 3dos extension from the Chrome Web Store: 3dos Extension
    Open the extension in the extension manager.

  5. Click the extension and paste your API key.

Depined

  1. Click this link: Depined Dashboard
  2. Register an account.
  3. After signing up, in the dashboard look for "Open Chrome Extension". If you cannot find it, download the extension from: Depined Extension
  4. Open the extension via Chrome’s extension manager and click on Depined.
  5. Click Log In and then click the extension again.

Note: You will earn points if your PC is active. Approximately 500 points equals $8-9, and you can earn up to 600-700 points daily.
